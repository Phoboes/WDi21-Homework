Fantasia Apocalyptica

Fantasia Apocalyptica is a multimedia work (in progress) for pipe organ, accompanied by four video tracks. It can be regarded as a somewhat literal translation of the Biblical book of Revelation into music.

Don wanted to create music based on the numbers and other mystical symbols as they appear in the bible, in the book of revelations.

Here is a video of an organist playing some of the composition.

Don will release the finshed product on his 80th birthday next year.
