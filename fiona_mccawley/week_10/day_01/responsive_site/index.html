Donald is the author of the series of books called The Art of Computer Programming.

He also created the software TeX, to allow anybody to produce high-quality books using minimal effort, and to provide a system that would give exactly the same results on all computers, at any point in time

He is so much more than those 2 paragraphs though, he also is in the midst of creating a multimedia work called Fantasia Apocalyptica. Which he intends to premiere to the world on his 80th Birthday next year.
